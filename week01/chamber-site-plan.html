Indianola Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Indianola Chamber of Commerce. This is the name of the town the chamber serves.

Site Purpose

The Indianola Chamber of Commerce serves Indianola and the surrounding communitites throughout the greater Des Moines area. Our goals include promoting sustainable business and community growth in the area, and helping to develop community policies that support the local economy. The Indianola Chamber of Commerce site purpose is to support the goals of the Indianola Chamber of Commerce.
